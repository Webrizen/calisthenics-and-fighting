Calisthenics and Fighting Skills Schedule

Morning Routine (No Equipment)

Warm-Up:

  • Jogging in place: 5-10 minutes
  • Dynamic stretches: Arm circles, leg swings, torso twists

Calisthenics:

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of knee push-ups or wall push-ups (8-10 reps)
  • Bodyweight squats: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 8-12 reps per leg
  • Plank: Hold for 20-30 seconds, 3 sets
  • Bicycle crunches: 3 sets of 10-15 reps per side

Fighting Skills Practice:

  • Shadowboxing
  • Footwork drills
  • Visualization

Evening Routine (Calisthenics Only)

Warm-Up:

  • Skipping rope: 5-10 minutes
  • Dynamic stretches: Arm swings, leg swings, hip circles

Calisthenics:

  • Pull-ups: 3 sets, aiming for 5-8 reps
  • Chin-ups: 3 sets, aiming for 5-8 reps
  • Dips: 3 sets, aiming for 5-8 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets, aiming for max reps
  • Leg raises: 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Fighting Skills Practice:

  • Shadowboxing
  • Footwork drills
  • Defensive drills
