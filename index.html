Calisthenics and Fighting Skills Schedule

Morning Routine (No Equipment)

Warm-Up:

  • Jogging in place: 5-10 minutes
  • Dynamic stretches: Arm circles, leg swings, torso twists

Calisthenics:

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of knee push-ups or wall push-ups (8-10 reps)
  • Bodyweight squats: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 8-12 reps per leg
  • Plank: Hold for 20-30 seconds, 3 sets
  • Bicycle crunches: 3 sets of 10-15 reps per side

Fighting Skills Practice:

  • Shadowboxing: Visualize and practice punches, kicks, and defensive movements
  • Footwork drills: Practice moving in and out of stances, shifting weight, and pivoting
  • Visualization: Imagine scenarios and plan your responses, enhancing reaction time

Evening Routine (Calisthenics and Kung Fu)

Warm-Up:

  • Skipping rope: 5-10 minutes
  • Dynamic stretches: Arm swings, leg swings, hip circles

Calisthenics (Using Bars):

  • Pull-ups: 3 sets, aiming for 5-8 reps
  • Chin-ups: 3 sets, aiming for 5-8 reps
  • Dips: 3 sets, aiming for 5-8 reps
  • Leg raises: 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Kung Fu Practice:

  • Stances: Practice horse stance, bow stance, and cat stance to build stability and strength
  • Basic Strikes: Practice punches (jab, cross) and palm strikes for power and precision
  • Kicks: Practice front kick, side kick, and roundhouse kick for balance and flexibility
  • Forms: Practice a basic kung fu form or sequence of movements to improve flow and coordination
